Process

Outdoor Process

Unique Furniture's exclusive furnishings for outdoor living offers consumers a wide range of styles, colors, and materials at affordable prices. All furniture features rust proof aluminum and weather resistant, hand woven, resin wicker. And to minimize environmental impact, all materials are 100% recyclable.

Aluminum frames are heliarc welded, by hand, and polished smooth. Each frame is individually powder coated, giving the frame strength and durability.

Superior-quality polyethylene-based resin wicker is characterized by high flexibility and tensile strength, similar to natural rattan. Richly colored throughout, smudge-proof, fade proof, and resistant to temperature variations, UFI's resin wicker is designed to last a lifetime.

UFI supplies both in-house and OEM designs, tailored to suit each clients' need. To find out more about these services, please contact us.
