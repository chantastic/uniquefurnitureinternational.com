About

Unique Furniture International has been in the industry for over 20 years, providing quality furniture for home and hospitality. Over the past few years UFI has made a steady and progressive transition from home furniture to hospitality. UFI's greatest strengths have always been in contract furniture. Specializing specifically in high-quality outdoor case goods, UFI delivers products with class, functionality, and durability - designed to look and perform well in the most aggressive of outdoor situations.
